Junior Electrical Engineer, Power Systems

Location

On site, Guildford, England

Summary

As a Junior Electrical Engineer, Power Systems, you are designing, prototyping, debugging and polishing electronic components that manage, control and deliver high voltage and/or high current power systems. These are key components of mobile robotics systems such as UAVs, UGV, USVs and their accessories.

Duties

  • Design Schematics
  • Design PCB layouts
  • Drive the execution of prototypes
  • Assemble, eventually rework and test the PCBs
  • Design assembly and test jigs
  • Polish design to production grade

Required Skills

  • Comprehensive knowledge of power electronics
  • Proficiency in EDA or ECAD
  • Proficiency in KiCAD, Fusion/Eagle and/or Altium
  • Good understanding of global procurement supply chain in regards to high power electronics assemblies and components

Nice to have

  • Prior relevant experience
  • Project portfolio of relevant parts/assemblies
